Full Page Review Notes:

Hero Section: Looks excellent - warm marketplace image, dark overlay, white text with good contrast. CTA buttons visible.

Product Grid: 4-column layout on desktop, 8 products displayed. Generated images (speaker, bag, mugs, skincare) look great.
The Unsplash images for watch, earbuds, candles, and lip gloss are loading but some look off-brand (earbuds has red/pink bg, candles shows a kitchen).
These are minor issues - the user can replace with their own product images.

About Section: "How It Works" 3-step process looks clean. "Ready to Order?" CTA banner in teal gradient is effective.

Footer: Simple and clean with store name, Telegram link, and navigation.

Floating Telegram Button: Visible and well-positioned in bottom-right.

Issues to address: The Unsplash images for some products don't match the warm marketplace aesthetic perfectly, but this is expected since the user will replace with their own product photos.

Overall: The website is functional, well-designed, and ready for customization.
